Coronavirus and Twitter: Tweets from the CDC, Fox5NY, and the Seattle Times

By Erika Wheless

May 20, 2020

As the coronavirus has made it's way across the US, Twitter has become a hub for fast changing updates on the virus. I looked at three accounts - the CDC, the Seattle Times, and Fox 5 NY - to compare their topo three retweets, and most commonly tweeted words. I scrapped the tweets using workbenchdata.com.
